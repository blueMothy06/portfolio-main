Hey there!

My work is motivated by a passion to design for everyone so that everyone has an equitable experience with my products. Not everyone has the same access to the digital world as I do, so I take that into consideration whenever I start a new project. As the world grows more dependent on digital spaces, I become more conscious of how my work can be accessible and usable for my audiences.

In my past, I've created my fair share of websites ever since I learned how to program at the age of 15. I've played around with my limited experience by creating games in HTML, CSS, and JS, rethinking the way those languages can be used.

During my time at San Jose State University, I earned my degree in Digital Media Art.
