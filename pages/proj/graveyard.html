Graveyard Shift

(2023)

A game where a tired overworked retail worker needs to fight her way out a ghost-infested mall.

This is a top down shooter game that I worked on with Julee Nhek (map and UI designer) and Madison Kolley (programmer). I drew the assets from the sprites, mall objects, and title/ending pages.

You, too, can fight ghosts.

Tool: Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro
