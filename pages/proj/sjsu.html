SJSU College of Graduate Studies Posters

(2022-2023)

When I was working at the College of Graduate Studies, I was tasked with creating several graphics for the department. I balanced the offical colors, blue and yellow, keeping the spirit of the school in each piece, while trying to keep the composition visually appealing. All of them were printed out and displayed on hallways, with the exception of the last design, which became a six-foot tall banner.

Tool: Adobe Illustrator
