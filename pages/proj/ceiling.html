i want a ceiling plant holder

(2022)

A silly game about wanting a ceiling plant holder, but not wanting to leave your house to get one. Expanded upon the framework I had created from i guess it's the Calamity now? where I continued my journey of making another point-and-click game. This one used more HTML and Javascript, where I created an array of text that the player could cycle through to create the essence of a story. The p5.JS aspect was also expanded upon with a few minigames tossed in.

You, too, can experience a deep yearning for a ceiling plant holder.

Tool: HTML, CSS, p5.JS, Adobe Photoshop
